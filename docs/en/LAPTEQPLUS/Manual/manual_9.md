# 9 Storage

* Clean and dry all parts and store them in a clean and dry place.
* If you do not use the device for a long time or are transporting it, use the original packaging.

!!! abstract "Attention!"
    **Risk of damage to the appliance!**

    High temperatures can cause the display to malfunction, damage the device or cause the battery to explode.

    Do not store the device in very hot places such as the interior of a car in summer or places exposed to direct sunlight.
