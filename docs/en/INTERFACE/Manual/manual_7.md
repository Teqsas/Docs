# 7 Help/Service Center

!!! warning "Caution!"
    **Possible risk of injury!**

    Improper repairs can cause your device to stop working safely. You are endangering yourself and those around you.

Often it is only small mistakes that lead to a malfunction. Most of the time, you can easily fix them yourself. By resetting the device, see [Reset of the interface](manual_5.md#53-reset-of-the-interface), any malfunctioning functions can be fixed.

Please note that improper repairs will also void the warranty claim and you may incur additional costs.
