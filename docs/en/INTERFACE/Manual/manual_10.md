# 10 Disposal

The packaging is made of eco-friendly materials that you can dispose of at the local recycling points.

Appliances marked with the symbol on the right must not be disposed of with household waste. You are obliged to dispose of such waste electrical and electronic equipment separately.

You can find out how to dispose of the old equipment from your municipal or city administration.

With separate disposal, you send the old equipment for recycling or other forms of reuse. In this way, they help to prevent potentially harmful substances from entering the environment.
