# 6 Cleaning

!!! danger "Danger!"
    **Danger to life from electric shock!**

    Disconnect the XLR cable to the display module (power supply) before carrying out any work on the device.

!!! abstract "Notice!"
    **Risk of damage to the appliance!**

    Do not immerse the device in water when cleaning, as water can penetrate the electrical system and damage it.

!!! abstract "Notice!"
    **Possible product damage!**

    The surface can be damaged.
    Do not use scratching or abrasive objects or aggressive cleaning agents for cleaning.

* Use a soft, lint-free cleaning cloth dampened with mild soapy water to clean the housing.
* Avoid scrubbing motions on the display window and on the glass window covering the laser outlet.
* Do not use chemicals, cleaning agents or solvents. This can lead to discolouration and corrosion of the outside of the device or result in an electric shock or fire.
